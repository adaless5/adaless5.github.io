What I do

I develop games! I am both an Algonquin College Game Development and Western University Communications graduate. I primarily develop in C++ and C# and have made projects in both Unreal and Unity Engine. I do everything from develop unique gameplay logic and elegant in-game systems to shaders and special effects using Unity and Unreal's built in systems. I'm great at debugging and solving compile, runtime and logical errors. I have experience with project management and employing the agile methodology to game development using software like Assembla and Github to maintain an organized project and task list. I am a very driven and self-motivated programmer and would love to work with you!
